Lasagna

Here's what you need:

  • 1 large white onion (diced)
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatos
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans of tomato paste
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 30 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup FRESHLY grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to follow:

  1. Stir in sugar, fresh basil, oregano, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and chopped parsley. Pour in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce. Stir well. Reduce heat to low and simmer 1-4 hours, stir occasionally.
  2. place lasagna noodles into the bottom of a pan. Pour hot tap water directly over the noodles.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes to allow cheese to brown. Serve hot!
