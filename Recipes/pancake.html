Pancakes

Here's what you need:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon vetetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven 200 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together milk, oil and egg.
  3. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium.
  4. For each pancake, spoon 2 to 3 tablespooms of batter onto skillet using the back of the spoon to spread the batter into a round shape.
  5. Cook until surface of pancakes have bubbles, then flip carefully and let cook for 1 to 2 more minutes.
