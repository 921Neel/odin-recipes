Rigatoni

Here's what you need:

  • 2 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup onion FINELY chopped
  • 2 teaspoon garlic
  • 24 oz jar of marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 oz of rigatoni pasta
  • 2 cups mozzerella cheese
  • Cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 defrees F.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the marinara sauce and tomato sauce to the pan and bring the sauce to a simmer.
  4. Toss the cooked rigatoni with the sauce
  5. Let stand for 5 minutes, then serve.
